Frame Rules

Restraint

Do

  • Start on warm parchment; add at least one sun bloom for atmosphere.
  • Set every line in ink — display, body, label, mono; contrast is size, not color.
  • Use Instrument Serif 400, tight line-height, negative tracking, for display.
  • Make every separator a 1px ink hairline; flood a yellow panel for poster moments.
  • Reserve JetBrains Mono for dates, numerals, and the bottom-right pagenum.

Don't

  • No drop shadows, no rounded corners, no bordered cards.
  • No second text color; no bold Instrument Serif.
  • No borders thicker than 1px; no inverted ink grounds.
  • No mono for body or headlines; no font substitutes.
  • Don't crowd the canvas — sparse reads as elegant.
